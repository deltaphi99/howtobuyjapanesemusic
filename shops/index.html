    Amazon Japan no Express shipping with tracking is the shipping method offered.

    Suruga-ya yes Good prices on used items.

Luckily, since Mercari can't really enforce this ban, there are proxies that can still buy anything from Mercari, even the items that are blocked on Buyee: Treasure Japan, vv.Japan, Neokyo, Japonica Market, and Goody-Japan are a few examples.
